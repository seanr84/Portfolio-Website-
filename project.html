Project 2 - Scrolling Shooter Game

A scrolling shooter gamme that I made developed in one of game development courses that I took. The goal is to move around and shoot enemy planes to increase the score.

The player can also take damage as indicated by the green health bar at the top left and the player can also gather life packs to gain back health lost.

Project 3 - Platform Game

This platform game allows the player to move around on different platforms to finish the level by going to the level sign to advance or win the game.

The player can destroy enemies by jumping on top of them to gain points, however the player will loose a life that are on the top right if they get hit by one.

There are other aspects to notice such as the vines that the player can climb up and down on, and the mushrooms that give the player an extra life.

Project 4 - Jams Discord Music Bot

This project is a music bot that plays music based on data it will find on YouTube.

Jams is responsive to user inputs by using slash commands such as "/play" to play a certain song or video. It will gather the video based on the title given by the user or the YouTube URL.

Project 5 - Personal Trainer Application (In Progress)

The intended goal for this project as it continues to be developed is to be a personal trainer app. A family relative is a personal trainer that has recorded videos about strength training, cardio, and overall fitness advice that will be implemented on the app based on the users preferrences and goals.

Based on those goals and certain requests, there will be a workout plan selected for the user also including a list of videos for them to follow. The app will also have the user track their workout for each planned workout day to track their progress.
